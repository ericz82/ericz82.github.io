Born and raised in sunny San Diego, I grew up swimming, eating tacos, and taking weekly hikes along Torrey Pines! At night, I dream about returning home and throwing a bonfire on the beach with my friends. That and a trip to Balboa Park.

The Bishop's School - Class of 2013

I graduated as a Knight from The Bishop's School in La Jolla in 2013. In addition to achieving in classes, I participated in Academic League, swam on the varisity swim team, and performed in school musicals. During summers I volunteered as a summer camp counselor at the Reuben H. Fleet Science Center.

Camp Hope - Kunming, China

The summer after graudating high school, I traveled to Kunming, Yunnan Province in China to work with a free non-profit summer camp. Many poorer families in rural parts of China do not adhere to the One-Child Policy, so second-born children do not have registration or hukou (戶口) in order to attend school. Camp Hope is a way to allow these children to enjoy the experience of being in a classroom, making friends, and most importantly hope in a future through education. Through teaching music, crafts, drama and english, I experienced from the brightness and eagerness to learn in these kids a joy incomparable to anything before.

University of California, Berkeley - Class of 2017

I am currently a third year student pursuing a double major in statistics and computer science. Outside of classes, I directed a troupe in Asian American themed drama and served as historian for Theatre Rice. I am currently a graphic and web designer for Innovative Design, an on-campus student-run organization that services the surrounding community through design, photography, and videography. I also work at the campus Student Learning Center as a tutor in math and statistics.

Projects

Trip Finder

Using Java, implemented a graph package and trip finder application that utilizes graph package. Trip finder takes in network of roads and intersections, starting location and destination. Outputs step by step driving directions of shortest route to destination.

Relational Database Management System

Using Java, implemented a miniature DBMS consisting of labeled columns of information. Users communicate with database via unique query language. Supports loading and saving of .db files.

Petting Zoo

Using Ruby on Rails, implemented a web application that allows users to interact with animals added by an admin "zookeeper" and view fellow users' interactions. App utilizes 'devise' gem for user authentication and 'cancancan' gem for adminstrator authorization.

Pokémon Smartwatch App

Android application on mobile and wearable that notifies users when they are geographically near a pokémon and prompts them to capture it. Users can view, feed and interact with captured pokémon on their mobile. Utilized Google Maps API for displaying locations where pokémon were captured.
